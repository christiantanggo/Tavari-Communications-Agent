# User Guide

## Getting Started

### 1. Sign Up
1. Go to the signup page
2. Enter your email and password
3. Fill in your business information:
   - Business name
   - Phone number
   - Address
   - Timezone
4. Click "Create Account"

### 2. Complete Setup
After signing up, you'll be guided through a 5-step setup process:

**Step 1: Business Information**
- Verify your business details
- Update if needed

**Step 2: Business Hours**
- Set your regular operating hours for each day
- Mark days as closed if needed

**Step 3: Holiday Hours**
- Add special hours for holidays
- Set holidays as closed
- Add custom hours for specific dates

**Step 4: FAQs**
- Add frequently asked questions
- The AI will use these to answer caller questions

**Step 5: Phone Number**
- Select an area code
- System will provision a phone number for your AI agent

### 3. Activate Your AI Agent
Once setup is complete, your AI agent will be automatically activated. You'll receive a phone number that callers can use to reach your AI assistant.

## Dashboard

### Overview
The dashboard shows:
- **Minutes Used**: Current usage vs. your plan limit
- **Recent Calls**: Latest calls handled by the AI
- **Recent Messages**: Messages taken from callers
- **AI Status**: Whether your AI is active

### Quick Actions
- **Rebuild Agent**: Update the AI with latest settings
- **Settings**: Configure your business and AI
- **Billing**: Manage your subscription

## Settings

### Business Information
- Update business name, phone, address
- Change timezone
- Update contact email

### Business Hours
- Modify regular hours
- Set days as closed
- Update holiday hours

### AI Settings
- **Personality**: Choose friendly, professional, casual, or formal
- **Voice**: Select AI voice (Alloy, Echo, Fable, Onyx, Nova, Shimmer)
- **Opening Greeting**: Customize how the AI greets callers
- **Ending Greeting**: Customize how the AI ends calls
- **FAQs**: Add, edit, or remove frequently asked questions

### Notifications
- **Email Notifications**: Toggle email summaries
- **SMS Notifications**: Toggle SMS for urgent callbacks
- **Test Notifications**: Send test emails/SMS to verify settings

## Calls

### View Call History
1. Go to "Calls" in the dashboard
2. See all calls handled by your AI
3. Click on a call to see details:
   - Caller information
   - Call duration
   - Transcript
   - AI summary
   - Intent (callback, message, etc.)

### Call Details
Each call shows:
- **Date & Time**: When the call occurred
- **Caller Number**: Phone number of the caller
- **Duration**: Length of the call
- **Transcript**: Full conversation transcript
- **Summary**: AI-generated summary
- **Intent**: What the caller wanted (callback, message, question, etc.)
- **Message Taken**: Whether a message was created

## Messages

### View Messages
1. Go to "Messages" in the dashboard
2. See all messages taken by the AI
3. Messages show:
   - Caller name and phone
   - Message text
   - Date and time
   - Read/unread status

### Mark as Read
- Click on a message to view details
- Unread messages are highlighted
- Mark messages as read when you've handled them

## Billing

### View Usage
- See current month's usage
- View minutes used vs. plan limit
- See when minutes reset

### Manage Subscription
- View current plan
- Upgrade or downgrade plan
- Access billing portal to:
  - Update payment method
  - View invoices
  - Cancel subscription

### Plan Options
- **Starter**: $79/month - 250 minutes, 5 FAQs
- **Core**: $129/month - 500 minutes, 10 FAQs
- **Pro**: $179/month - 750 minutes, 20 FAQs

### Minutes Exhaustion Settings
Choose what happens when you run out of minutes:
- **Disable AI**: AI turns off, calls forward to your business
- **Allow Overage**: Continue using AI, pay for overage minutes (with monthly cap)

## Troubleshooting

### AI Not Answering Calls
1. Check if AI is enabled in settings
2. Verify phone number is provisioned
3. Click "Rebuild Agent" to refresh settings
4. Check dashboard for any error messages

### Calls Not Showing Up
1. Wait a few minutes (processing may take time)
2. Refresh the dashboard
3. Check if webhook is configured correctly
4. Contact support if issue persists

### Settings Not Updating
1. Click "Rebuild Agent" after making changes
2. Wait a few minutes for changes to take effect
3. Test with a call to verify

### Billing Issues
1. Check billing portal for subscription status
2. Verify payment method is up to date
3. Check email for billing notifications
4. Contact support for assistance
